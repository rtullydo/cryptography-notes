Algorithm 2.6.2. Fast inverse mod \(p\).
\(a \in \F_p\ad\text{.}\)
  1. Since \(a\) then must be smaller than \(p\text{,}\) \(p \nmid a\text{,}\) and so \(a^{p-1} = 1\) in \(\F_p\ad\text{.}\)
  2. But then \(a^{p-2} \equiv a\inv\text{.}\)
  3. Apply the fast powering algorithm to compute \(a^{p-2}\text{.}\) The result is \(a\inv\text{.}\)
